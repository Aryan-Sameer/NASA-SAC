Hydrospera

Hydrospera, a hypothetical habitable exoplanet planet revolving around a G-type star 'Rubysol'. Almost half of the planet surface is covered with liquid water enabling the possibility of life.

The atmosphere is made of various gases like nitrogen, Oxygen, etc. scattering the golden light from the host star.

Learn more

Hydrospera is a super Earth exoplanet that orbits a G-type star. the mass is 1.2 X Earth. It takes 222 days to complete one orbit around the star, and is 0.86 AU from its star.
